Introduction

GDP is very important in every contries economy. GDP can calculated be using expenditure, production or incomes, In this project, it will focus on expenditure. G20 countries's GDP it is very important in the world.

The G20 (or Group of Twenty) is an international forum for the governments and central bank governors from 19 countries and the European Union (EU).

This project will analyze the correlation between GDP and the expenditure on education, healthcare, and military, among ten countries in G20, including Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, India, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

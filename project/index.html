Introduction

The G20 (or Group of Twenty) is an international forum for the governments and central bank governors from 19 countries and the European Union (EU).

G20 is the premier forum for international cooperation on the most important aspects of the international economic and financial agenda. It brings together the world's major advanced and emerging economies.

Analysis in this project select ten countries from G20. Those ten contries are Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, India and United Kingdom, United States

Analysis will focus on Education, Healthcare Military Spending and GDP, we will also see the relationship between them.
